bio

A group of teenagers straight out of a small studio in Surrey, just on the edge of London, creating unique and timeless music. Formed in 2018, The Circuits have grown and developed their style and are now in the studio recording their best music. Their smooth vocals, dreamy keys, punchy guitar and impactful rhythm section is the definition of The Circuits.
